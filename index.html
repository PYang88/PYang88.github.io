About Us

Japanese cusine is centuries old, and contains a wide variety of many fragrant, vibrant flavors. Shintaro focuses on delivering an authentic experience of Japanese cuisine. By using only the freshest ingredients, we can maintain a high standard for our food. Our chefs, who have spent years prefecting their craft, take great care and pride in their work. They welcome you to come in and enjoy their food.
